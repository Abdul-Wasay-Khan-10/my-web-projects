Welcome to Game Of Sultans

Game of Sultans" invites players to rule the Ottoman Empire strategically, while also delving into the dynamics of a royal harem for a comprehensive gaming experience.

Combining historical simulation and personal interactions, "Game of Sultans" empowers players to guide their empire's path in the Ottoman era, intertwining strategic decisions with intimate relationships.

Gaming Experience


Historical Simulation & Strategy


"Game of Sultans" is set in the Ottoman Empire during its height, where players take on the role of a sultan, managing various aspects of an empire. The game combines historical elements with strategy and simulation gameplay. Players are responsible for managing the empire's economy, diplomacy, military campaigns, and harem.


Harem Management


A unique aspect of the game is the harem management system. Players can recruit and interact with consorts, each of whom has their own attributes and abilities. Developing relationships with consorts can provide benefits and bonuses to the player's empire. The harem aspect adds a personal and interpersonal dimension to the gameplay.


Decision-Making and Customization


The game involves decision-making that affects various aspects of the empire, including alliances, wars, resource management, and more. Players can customize their sultan's appearance, abilities, and strategies. The choices players make throughout the game can impact the empire's success, alliances, and the overall story progression.
